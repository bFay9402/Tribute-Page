Satoru Iwata

A treasure in gaming

A look into Satoru Iwata's life

  • Born December 6, 1959 in Sapporo, Japan
  • 1974 - got his first programmable calculator
  • 1975 - began developing his own games
  • 1978 - he obtained his first computer, a Commodore PET
  • 1978 - began to study computer science at Tokyo Institute of Technology
  • 1980 - joins HAL Labratory, Inc. as a part time programmer
  • 1982 - becomes full time employee and only programmer at HAL
  • 1983 - became the companies coordinator of software production
  • 1993 - Iwata becomes President of HAL
  • 2000 - takes a position at Nintendo as the head of its corporate planning division and seat on the board of directors
  • 2002 - becomes president of Nintendo after former president Yamauchi retires
  • 2005 - revitilaized Nintendo's handheld market with the Nintendo DS
  • 2006 - Iwata put focus on development of products that improved quality of life starting with the Wii
  • 2012 - Wii U released as a failure, Iwata took a pay cut due to its failure
  • 2013 - took additional role as Nintendo of America's CEO
  • Died July 11, 2015 age 55

More info about Satoru Iwata can be found on his Wikipedia page.
